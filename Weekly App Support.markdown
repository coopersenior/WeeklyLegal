# Weekly App Support

Welcome to the support page for *Weekly.* This guide provides answers to common questions, troubleshooting tips, and ways to get in touch with our support team.

## Getting Started

*Weekly* helps you stay up to date with your close friends by encouraging you to post at least once a week. Friends new posts after the reset date are blurred until you post yourself. 

### Key Features

- **Post photos**: Share what you have been up to with your friends.
- **Likes**: Like your friend's posts.
- **Comments**: Comment on your friend's posts.
- **Shares**: Share a friend's post in a direct message to another user.
- **Messaging**: Direct message with other users on the app.
- **Posts are blurred**: *Weeky* encourages you to post once a week. If you don't post after the reset day new friend's posts will be blurred until you share a post!

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### How do I delete my account?

- Go to *Profile* &gt; *Settings &gt; Submit Feedback/Bug Report* to submit a ticket.

### Can I sync Weekly across multiple devices?

- Yes! Ensure you're signed in with the same account on all devices. .

### How do I reset my password?

1. On the login screen, tap *Forgot Password?*
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.

### How do I block a user?

- Go to *Profile* &gt; *Settings &gt; Blocked Users* and search for the user you would like to block. Blocked users wont show up anywhere on the app and they won't be able to see any of your information. To unblock a user go back into the *Blocked Users* page and select unblock. 

## Troubleshooting

### App Crashes or Freezes

- **Check for Updates**: Ensure you're using the latest version of *Weekly*. Visit your app store to update.
- **Restart the App**: Close and reopen the app.
- **Reinstall**: Uninstall and reinstall the app, ensuring you're signed in to restore your data.
